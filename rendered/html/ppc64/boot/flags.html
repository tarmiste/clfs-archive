7.20. Build Flags

We will need to copy our build variables into our new system: 

cat >> ${CLFS}/root/.bash_profile << EOF
export BUILD32="${BUILD32}"
export BUILD64="${BUILD64}"
export CLFS_TARGET32="${CLFS_TARGET32}"
EOF
